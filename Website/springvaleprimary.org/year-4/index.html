Year 4

Welcome to our Year 4 Class Page!

Miss Alderson, Mrs Gale and Mrs Akrill are looking forward to a fantastic year with Year 4.

Our Curriculum is listed below:

Long term planning cycle B Y3-4

Year 4 will need to be times tables wizards this year as they are the first class to complete the official online times table assessment (2020 was cancelled due to Covid.) Please make sure your child is accessing TT rock stars and practising a soundcheck quiz to know the times tables with confidence.

The importance of reading for children cannot be underestimated. Reading for pleasure can benefit a child’s education, social and cognitive development, their wellbeing, and their mental health. Please make sure your child is reading a variety of reading materials (books, comics/magazines, newspaper articles) at home and completing their reading diaries to earn merits.

PE kits should be brought into school on a Monday and taken home weekly to be cleaned.

Children will have a weekly spelling test on a Monday morning.

Topics for Fantastic Flemings this year are as follows

Autumn 1: Forces and Magnets

Autumn 2: Savage Stone Age

Spring: Vile Victorians

Summer 1: Rotten Romans

Summer 2: Bellisimo- Passport to Italy
